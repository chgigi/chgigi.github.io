About Me


Hey! I'm Charles Ginane. I am an IT engineer specialized in image processing and research and development. I did my formation to the school EPITA during my five years in the image processing specialisation. This specialisation develop my skills and methods on image and video processing (image recognition, image reconstruction and image segmentation), on image synthesis (image reproduction, video animation) and artificial intelligence appliqued to image (image dectection, classification)


During my last two years, I worked at the LRDE (the main research laboratory of EPITA). I worked on a project in Python to generate videos in marine environments by using the MAYA API (An 3D generation software). The goal is to generate those videos to train an artificial interlligence which detects any danger in marine environment like colision or man at the sea. This dataset of video need to have a lot of parameters like weather, time, objects and context. With a Python library and a lot of 3D model of (boats, buoy, ...), I provide a simply way to generate video with different parameters.

More precisely, I develop an artificial intelligence which detects any dangers in the marine environment. My favorites languages are Python, C++ and C. I work on UNIX and Windows OS. You can contact me for more information by sending me an email or click on below links:
